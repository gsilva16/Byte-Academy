About

Have you ever wanted to play some pick-up on campus, but did not know where to go or if there were others that also wanted to play?
Well, we are here to solve that problem for you. With this app you will be able to see what is going on around you, what is being played, where, and also when.
Don't see the sport you want? Can't make any of the times? No worries, you can always create your own events and have people show up.
We want to make sure you can always find something to do and Game On!
